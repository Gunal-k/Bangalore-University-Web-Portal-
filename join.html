Admission Information

Eligibility

  1. The candidates should have passed either X Class (SSLC/CBSE/ICSE Board) or equivalent examination recognized by the Dept. Of Pre-University Education, Karnataka.
  2. Candidates from states outside Karnataka are governed by the conditions specified in G.O.No.Ed/45/SHH-95 dated 25-5-95, Bangalore.
  3. Admission of foreign nationals is subject to fulfilment of:
    • Eligibility requirement of the Director PU Education, Bangalore (Candidates coming from abroad shall be admitted to the course only on the production of a provisional eligibility certificate, issued by the department, attested by a Gazetted Officer).
    • No Objection Certificate from the Ministry of Human Resource Development (Department of Education) Government of India.
    • Valid passport and study visa for the study in the institution.
    • AIDS-free certificate.

Selection

  1. Merit Based
  2. The applicant should satisfy all other conditions set by the Karnataka PU Board.

Students willing to apply must

  1. Visit the college with their parents and request a Registration/Application form.
  2. Submit the duly-filled form along with necessary documents as mentioned.

Documents

Along with the application, one copy each of the relevant documents mentioned below must be submitted. At the time of admission interview, all the originals along with another set of photocopies of the same have to be produced.

  1. Transfer Certificate from the last attended school.
  2. Class X Marks Card.
  3. Conduct Certificate.
  4. SC/ST/Other backward class/Minority Certificates.
  5. 6 passport-size and 4 stamp-size photographs.

Fee Regulations

  1. Fees once paid will not be refunded.
  2. A student joining the college after the commencement of the academic year shall pay the full fees for the year.
  3. Students are required to pay fees for the next higher class on the date and time intimated to them by the College Office.

General Norms

  1. Application for Admission should be in the prescribed form issued by the College Office for a payment of a nominal fee.
  2. Applications should be submitted personally by the candidates. Incomplete applications will not be processed.
  3. One photocopy each of all relevant certificates is to be submitted along with the application form. No original certificates should be attached to the application form. Reservation category candidates have to submit the photocopy of the caste/category certificate along with the application form.
  4. The applicant should be present for admission along with the parent/guardian.
  5. All original certificates along with photographs should be produced at the time of admission.
  6. The admission process includes payment of fees and campus culture briefing.
  7. Admissions are subject to the approval of the Karnataka PU Board.
  8. Admission to any course is for one academic year only.
  9. Students will be admitted to the higher class on the basis of a fresh application for admission, taking into account their progress in studies, regularity of attendance, conduct, and behavior during the previous year.
